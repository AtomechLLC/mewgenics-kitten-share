🐾 about the data — everything is decoded from your save, nothing invented
Names, stats and appearance are decoded from your save. Stats (7 core + HP) are real, verified values. Portraits use each cat's real data: the game's own idle-pose rig (from catanis.swf), the cat's real coat color (its palette row), and its real fur pattern (tabby/spots/stripes — the CatTexture sheet, tinted to the coat and clipped to each part). One approximation remains: SVG can't do the game's per-pixel UV sampling, so stripes render straight rather than wrapped to the body contour, but they read correctly as the cat's markings. Cats from an unrecognized build, or whose genes fail the range/symmetry check, render a neutral base kitten labelled "appearance unverified".
